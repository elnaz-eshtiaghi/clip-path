THERE'S NO SOBSTITUTE FOR POWER

EXCEPT MORE POWER.

What CrossFit has taught me, and what I think everyone can apply to their own fitness, is the idea that the body can always do more. The average CrossFit WOD is deliberately designed to fatigue your body to the point where you don't think you can go on. The lesson in the WODs is that when your body is totally exhausted, you must look for a different energysource. And guess what? Your body will do it.
